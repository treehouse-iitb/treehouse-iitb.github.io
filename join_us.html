Open Positions

We are looking for passionate and talented researchers to join us at Treehouse. Treehouse provides a highly interdisciplinary research environment. At Treehouse, you will have the opportunity to holistically develop your skills in research, scientific communication, and real-world problem-solving. You will also have the opportunity to build your network with other researchers in India and abroad.

Positions are currently open for Postdocs, PhD students, MTech students, and UG students to work on the following projects. You are also most welcome to bring your own project idea in a relevant theme.

  • Tree architecture and its environmental controls. Required skills: statistical analysis, R programming.
  • Evolution in continuously structured populations. Required skills: Strong background in Math and C++ programming.
  • Equilibrium solutions of Plant-FATE. Required skills: Strong background in math and C++ programming.
  • Plant rooting strategies. Required skills: C++ and R programming.
  • Graphical visualization of forest growth. Required skills: C++ and OpenGL programming.
  • Leaf water-potential sensor design. Required skills: Electronics and instrumentation.

If you are interested in joining Treehouse, please send us your CV and a brief statement of your interests to the contact email mentioned below.
